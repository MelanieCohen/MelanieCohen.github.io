About the Artist

Hi there !!! I'm an illustrator living and working out of Vancouver, BC and (occasionally) Durban, SA. I Enjoy tea, cartoons, Godzilla, and spend too much time with my cat. My primary focus is on children's books and media, and my practice is informed by nostalgia, memory, and my own childhood, as well as shape, form and contemporary toy design. I am a recent graduate of Emily Carr University of Art + Design with a major in illustration and print.
